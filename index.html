who am i

hello! i'm a software engineer, passionate about data analytics and machine learning. i'm in the 2nd year of the computer science masters program at stanford university and will graduate in june 2019. i'm also currently serving as the head of data & analytics for blocklight - an e-commerce analytics startup. i have a background in computer engineering (undergrad at purdue university); and i have experience in cyber security consulting (2+ years at deloitte) and experience with frontend and backend development (previous internships at qwerks and nielsen).

projects

during my 1st year at stanford i worked on a number of exciting machine learning projects; and like most software engineers, i'm usually tinkering with a few personal projects on the side. a goal of mine is to get better at cleanly packaging / publishing my work - this section will expand as i improve.

segmentation for satellite imagery

developed a U-Net convulutional neural net to detect features in satellite images; implemented with Keras, using a semi-supervised autoencoder for segmentation pretraining

cryptocurrency price prediction

developed a classifier to predict daily changes in coin prices using news and social media text sentiment; implemented with SciKit-Learn and TensorFlow

cryptocurrency portfolio management

developed a profitable portfolio management tool using daily market data; written in Python, using reinforcement learning to optimize an Markov decision process
